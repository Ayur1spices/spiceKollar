Origin & Cultivation

Flourishing in Rajakumari's unique microclimate, our pepper benefits from cool mountain air and rich volcanic soil. The high-altitude cultivation (800m above sea level) results in denser, more aromatic berries. Each vine is trained on indigenous support trees, and berries are harvested at the precise moment they blush red, then sun-dried on traditional bamboo mats to develop their signature bold flavor.
