Kollar Spices's journey began in 1967 with a modest spice business founded by Mr. Saju's father in a small Indian town. Initially focused on high-quality cardamom, the business grew by emphasizing purity and high-quality products.

Kollar Spices became a symbol of traditional excellence, maintaining a high-quality work ethic to all its stakeholders. The values passed down generations are one of hard work, vision, and commitment to sustainable practices.
